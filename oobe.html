OOBE

We helped OOBE captivate their audience with this minimalist and yet highly interactive responsive website that allows their stunning portfolio of work to really sing.

Delivering a responsive website that would do justice to such a beautiful body of work across desktop, tablet and mobile devices was a huge challenge for our web design team. We chose a clean, minimal approach in order to compliment such an eclectic collection of art, topped off with some lovely animated interactive details.
