Dene

Dene Films are a leading broadcast and commercial film company with offices in London and Newcastle. Despite 20 years and more than 4,000 productions under their belt, the sheer scale of their operation and the vast portfolio of award-winning work remained something of a secret.

Response to the website has been huge. In fact, we’ve had to upgrade the server twice to cope with the enormous hike in bandwidth, as improved search-engine visibility and user-subscriptions have resulted in a whopping 450% increase in traffic.

We designed and built an extensive portfolio website, providing quick and easy access to Dene's most valuable assets; their award-winning films.

We created video podcasts, allowing visitors to subscribe to the latest content from specific genres or directors, downloading new videos automatically to their computer as they’re released.
