Work flow

The key to a successful front of house operation is to establish a clear work flow and communication between the front of house staff and kitchen staff.

Take a look at this diagram to see the way in which kitchen staff and waiting staff can work together in a restaurant to serve customers. In the diagram, activities performed by kitchen staff are shown in green and waiting staff activities are shown in purple.

Activity

Now consider the work flow for a different type of food/drink establishment. For example, what do you think the work flow would look like in a high street fast food restaurant?

Have a go at drawing a diagram to show this work flow. You might find it useful to share your work with others in your class and/or your class teacher.
