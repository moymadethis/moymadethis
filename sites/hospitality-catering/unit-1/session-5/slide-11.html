Minimising security risks

A business may take the following actions when dealing with potential security risks:

  • Lock all doors and windows when the business is closed (front of house staff may often be the last to leave the premises, after clearing away tables, dishes, linen, etc)
  • The business may have a clear opening up/locking up procedure that staff must follow
  • Only certain members of staff may hold keys and be responsible for opening up/locking up
  • Provide staff with lockers to store their belonging during a shift
  • Install an alarm system (and perhaps also CCTV to monitor security risks)
  • All cash to be held in a safe or locked till until it can be taken to the bank
  • Security lighting may be used outside
  • If there are any fittings outside of the business (outdoor tables, chairs, etc), they may be securely fastened down
  • Regular staff training relating to security issues and how to deal with them
