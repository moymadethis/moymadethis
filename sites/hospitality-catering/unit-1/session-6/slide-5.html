Customer service

Customer service is all about the actions that a business takes in order to meet (and exceed) customer needs, requirements and expectations. When customer service is done well...

  • Customers will feel happy and satisfied
  • Customers will return to the business in the future
  • The business will increase sales and therefore make more money (this money can be used to grow and improve the business further)

Customers will tell others about their positive experience, building a positive reputation for the business

Think First

Think of a time when you have visited a hospitality/catering establishment and received good (or excellent) customer service. Type a description of this experience in the box below. What did staff members do to deliver good service? How did the service make you feel?

Here are just a few examples of what makes good customer service:

  • Friendly and polite staff
  • Helpful staff
  • Staff with good knowledge of products and services on offer
  • Smart and professional staff
  • Customers are made to feel welcomed and valued
  • Staff are able to deal with complaints/problems quickly and efficiently
  • Staff are willing to ‘go the extra mile’ for customers – they go beyond what is expected in order to create a great customer experience!
