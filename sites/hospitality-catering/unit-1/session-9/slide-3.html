Evaluating options

All of the information on the previous page can help you to identify and review suitable options when proposing the new hospitality and catering provision.

Once you have identified the options that are available, you can begin to review the options and hopefully identify one option that is most suitable.

You could begin by writing a summary of each option (this is a short description, which includes all of the key/relevant information about the option). Then, based on each summary, perhaps you can then identify the main advantages and disadvantages of each option.

Advantages are all of the potential positive points – Why is the option a good option? What could make this a successful provision?

Disadvantages would be the potentially negative points – Why would this option not be the right choice? What could go wrong with the provision?

Creating a SWOT analysis is a very useful tool that can help you to identify advantages and disadvantages. Move on to the next page to learn more.
