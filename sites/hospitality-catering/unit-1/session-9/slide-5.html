Making recommendations

Preparing for your assessment

In your assessment for this Unit, you will need to be able to demonstrate that you are able to:

  • Recommend options for hospitality provision

You’ll find lots of useful information over the following pages to help you complete this part of your assessment.

Once you have identified your options and their advantages/disadvantages, it can be helpful to put all of this information into a table – click to see an example.

You could also choose to add the following information to this table:

  • Factors that could affect the success of each option
  • The target customers for each option
  • The needs and expectations of these customers
  • The job roles that will need to be filled in each option
  • The health and safety actions required for each option

All of this information can help you to decide on the best option to recommend and it can also be used to support and justify your decision.

By showing that you have considered all of the key information during the decision making process, you are able to make a strong and structured recommendation, giving valid reasons for your choices.
