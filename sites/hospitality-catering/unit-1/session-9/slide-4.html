Now let’s take a look at an example to illustrate how you can put together your own SWOT analysis.

SWOT analysis example

Let’s imagine that you are reviewing the option of a new restaurant provision in a busy town centre. You have put together the following SWOT analysis to help you identify the advantages and disadvantages of this option.

Strengths: Weaknesses:
  • Location has good public transport links nearby
  • Building used to be a restaurant, so already has kitchen and serving facilities built in
  • Close to a business park, so well located for attracting office workers for lunch
  • Building has been empty for two years, so will need some repair and renovation work prior to opening
  • Limited car parking spaces nearby
  • Already a lot of restaurants open in town
Opportunities: Threats:
  • The second floor of the building could be developed into a bar and function room if the business grows
  • Town is very quiet during Winter months, which could affect profitability
  • There is another similar new restaurant opening in the town next month, which could reduce customer numbers
