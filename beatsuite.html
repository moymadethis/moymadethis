The BeatSuite

We helped the Beat Suite increase retention and loyalty for a second time with this enterprise-level e-commerce website and iTunes integration.

A lot can change in ten years when you’ve established an e-commerce model that works; business thrives, customers multiply and your product inventory swells to epic proportions. By the time we came to re-design this award-winning e-commerce website (we designed the first one back in 2002), the Beat Suite’s library had grown to more than 5,000 high quality music tracks and 20,000 active users, which meant that building for scale was going to be critical to the website’s success.
